Free Ontario G1 Practice Test

Our free G1 practice test is designed to help new drivers prepare for the Ontario written knowledge exam with no cost and no sign-up required. If you are at least 16 years old, you can visit a DriveTest centre and take the official G1 test, which includes 40 multiple-choice questions. The test is divided into two sections: 20 questions on road signs and 20 questions on rules of the road. To pass, you must correctly answer at least 16 questions in each section.

The real G1 test is based on the Ontario Driver’s Handbook, and our practice questions are designed to match the format and difficulty of the official exam. Ontario uses a graduated licensing system, meaning after you pass your G1 test, you must practice driving with a fully licensed driver and follow all G1 restrictions before moving on to your G2 licence.

Unlike other platforms, our practice tests are completely free and built to simulate the real testing experience. Many users find that practicing here helps them feel more confident and better prepared when they take the actual test at a DriveTest centre.
